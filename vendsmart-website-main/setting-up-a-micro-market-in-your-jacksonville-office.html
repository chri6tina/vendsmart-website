What is a Micro-Market?

A micro-market is a self-service convenience store that transforms your office break room into a modern retail experience. Unlike traditional vending machines, micro-markets offer fresh food, healthy options, and a seamless shopping experience with self-checkout technology.

Fresh Food Options

Salads, sandwiches, fresh fruit, and healthy snacks

Self-Checkout Technology

Touch-screen kiosks with contactless payment options

Increased Revenue

Higher sales compared to traditional vending machines

Employee Satisfaction

Better food options improve workplace morale
