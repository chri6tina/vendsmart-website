Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about manufacturing vending machines and our services

What types of products do you offer for manufacturing facilities?

We offer energy-boosting snacks, hydration solutions, quick meal options, and essential items for workers. Our selection is designed to support productivity and meet the needs of workers on all shifts.

How much does it cost to install vending machines in my facility?

We offer free installation and equipment placement for qualified manufacturing facilities. There are no upfront costs - we handle all installation, maintenance, and stocking. Revenue is shared between your facility and VendSmart.

Are your machines safe for industrial environments?

Yes! Our industrial vending machines are designed to meet OSHA safety standards and withstand harsh manufacturing environments. They feature durable construction, safety compliance, and emergency protocols.

How do you handle 24/7 shift operations?

Our machines are designed for 24/7 operation and we provide round-the-clock support. We monitor inventory remotely and restock based on usage patterns across all shifts, including overnight and weekend operations.

Can workers use their employee IDs or mobile payments?

Yes! Our machines accept credit cards, mobile payments, and can integrate with employee ID systems. We can also set up shift-based access and usage analytics for your facility management.

What if a machine breaks down during production hours?

We provide 24/7 emergency support and maintenance. If a machine malfunctions, we'll respond quickly to fix the issue. Our smart technology also alerts us to potential problems before they affect your workers.
