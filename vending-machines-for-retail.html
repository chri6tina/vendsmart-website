Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about retail vending machines and our services

What types of products do you offer for retail stores?

We offer a wide selection of snacks, beverages, convenience items, and impulse purchase products. Our selection is designed to complement your existing inventory and drive additional sales without competing with your core products.

How much does it cost to install vending machines in my store?

We offer free installation and equipment placement for qualified retail locations. There are no upfront costs - we handle all installation, maintenance, and stocking. Revenue is shared between your store and VendSmart.

Can customers use credit cards or mobile payments?

Yes! Our modern vending machines accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. This provides maximum convenience for your customers and increases sales potential.

Where should I place vending machines in my store?

Optimal locations include store entrances, rest areas, fitting rooms, and parking areas. We'll assess your store layout and recommend the best placement to maximize customer convenience and impulse purchases.

How often do you restock the machines?

We monitor inventory levels remotely and restock machines based on usage patterns. Typically, machines are restocked 2-3 times per week, but we can adjust frequency based on your store's traffic and sales volume.

Will vending machines compete with my existing products?

No, we carefully select products that complement your existing inventory rather than compete with it. Our vending machines provide additional convenience options that can actually increase overall customer satisfaction and dwell time.
