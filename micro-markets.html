What Are Micro-Markets?

Modern self-service convenience stores that transform your break room into a premium shopping experience.

Micro-markets are self-service convenience stores that provide employees with access to fresh food, healthy snacks, beverages, and everyday essentials. Unlike traditional vending machines, micro-markets offer a more sophisticated shopping experience with open-air displays, self-checkout kiosks, and a wider variety of products.

Open-air shopping experience
Multiple payment options
Fresh and healthy food options
Smart inventory management
