Jacksonville Vending Machines - DIY Troubleshooting Solutions

Jacksonville Vending Machines - Try these solutions before calling for professional help.

Payment System Solutions

1. Check Cash Acceptor

Ensure bills are not wrinkled or damaged. Clean the bill path with a soft cloth.

2. Reset Card Reader

Power cycle the machine to reset the card reader system.

3. Check Change Dispenser

Verify that the change dispenser is not jammed or empty.

Cooling System Solutions

1. Check Temperature Settings

Verify the thermostat is set to the correct temperature (usually 35-40°F).

2. Clean Condenser

Remove dust and debris from the condenser coils to improve cooling efficiency.

3. Check Airflow

Ensure nothing is blocking the air vents or cooling fans.

Dispensing Problems Solutions

1. Check Product Alignment

Ensure products are properly aligned in the vending mechanism.

2. Clear Jams

Carefully remove any stuck products from the dispensing area.

3. Reset Motors

Power cycle the machine to reset the vending motors.

Electrical Issues Solutions

1. Check Power Source

Verify the machine is properly plugged in and the outlet is working.

2. Reset Circuit Breaker

Check and reset the circuit breaker if the machine has tripped.

3. Power Cycle

Turn the machine off and on to reset the electrical system.
