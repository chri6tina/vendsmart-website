Jacksonville Vending Machines - Privacy Policy

Jacksonville Vending Machines - How we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Jacksonville Vending Machines (VendSmart) collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Contact us for quotes or services
  • Fill out contact forms on our website
  • Call our customer service line
  • Sign up for our services
  • Request maintenance or repairs

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, business name, and service requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide vending machine services and support
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send you quotes and proposals
  • Schedule installations and maintenance
  • Process payments and transactions
  • Improve our services and customer experience
  • Send you important updates about our services

3. Information Sharing

Jacksonville Vending Machines does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure data transmission protocols
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Employee training on data protection

5. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage
  • Improve our website functionality
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities

7. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@jacksonvillevendingmachines.com
  • Address: Jacksonville, Florida

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the effective date.
