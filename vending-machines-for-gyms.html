Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about gym vending machines and our services

What types of products do you offer for gyms?

We offer a comprehensive selection of fitness-focused products including protein shakes, energy bars, healthy snacks, sports drinks, bottled water, and fitness supplements. All products are carefully selected to support active lifestyles and fitness goals.

How much does it cost to install vending machines in my gym?

We offer free installation and equipment placement for qualified gyms. There are no upfront costs - we handle all installation, maintenance, and stocking. Revenue is shared between your gym and VendSmart.

Can members use credit cards or mobile payments?

Yes! Our modern vending machines accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. This provides maximum convenience for your members.

How often do you restock the machines?

We monitor inventory levels remotely and restock machines based on usage patterns. Typically, machines are restocked 2-3 times per week, but we can adjust frequency based on your gym's needs and member traffic.

Do you offer healthy and organic options?

Absolutely! We specialize in healthy vending and offer organic snacks, natural protein bars, fresh fruit options, and clean-label products. We can customize the selection to match your gym's health and wellness philosophy.

What if a machine breaks down?

We provide 24/7 support and maintenance. If a machine malfunctions, we'll respond quickly to fix the issue. Our smart technology also alerts us to potential problems before they affect your members.
