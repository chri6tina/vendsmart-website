Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about hotel vending machines and our services

What types of products do you offer for hotels?

We offer premium snacks, beverages, travel essentials, personal care items, and over-the-counter medications. Our selection is curated to meet the needs of hotel guests and maintain your property's quality standards.

How much does it cost to install vending machines in my hotel?

We offer free installation and equipment placement for qualified hotels. There are no upfront costs - we handle all installation, maintenance, and stocking. Revenue is shared between your hotel and VendSmart.

Can guests use credit cards or mobile payments?

Yes! Our modern vending machines accept credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash. This provides maximum convenience for your guests.

Where should I place vending machines in my hotel?

Optimal locations include lobbies, guest floors, near elevators, pool areas, and parking areas. We'll assess your property and recommend the best placement to maximize guest convenience and sales.

How often do you restock the machines?

We monitor inventory levels remotely and restock machines based on usage patterns. Typically, machines are restocked 2-3 times per week, but we can adjust frequency based on your hotel's occupancy and guest traffic.

Do you offer premium and organic options?

Absolutely! We offer premium snacks, organic options, gourmet coffee, and high-quality products that match your hotel's standards. We can customize the selection to align with your property's brand and guest expectations.
