Jacksonville Vending Machines - Two Decades of Excellence

Jacksonville Vending Machines by VendSmart was founded in 2004 with a simple mission: to revolutionize the vending experience in Jacksonville, Florida. What started as a small family business has grown into Jacksonville's premier vending machine company, serving over 50 businesses and maintaining more than 500 machines throughout the region.

Our journey began when we recognized that traditional vending machines were failing to meet the evolving needs of modern workplaces. We saw an opportunity to combine cutting-edge technology with exceptional service to create vending solutions that not only provide convenience but also enhance workplace satisfaction and productivity.

Today, Jacksonville Vending Machines stands as a testament to our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We've expanded our services to include smart vending machines, micro-markets, coffee services, and customized solutions that cater to the unique needs of each business we serve.
